BMR Calculator

Calculate your daily calorie expenditure to help plan your nutrition and achieve your fitness goals more effectively.

0
Calories/day (at rest)

This is your basic metabolic rate - the minimum calories needed when your body is at complete rest.

To determine your total daily energy expenditure, multiply your BMR by the appropriate activity factor:

Sedentary (little/no exercise): BMR × 1.2 = 0 calories

Lightly active (light exercise 1-3 days/week): BMR × 1.375 = 0 calories

Moderately active (moderate exercise 3-5 days/week): BMR × 1.55 = 0 calories

Very active (hard exercise 6-7 days/week): BMR × 1.725 = 0 calories

Extra active (very hard exercise & physical job): BMR × 1.9 = 0 calories
